Checkout as guest or register

Choose the pace that suits you. We keep checkout streamlined while encouraging students to create an account for an even smoother path.

Register and save time

Create an account to unlock the fastest way to reorder gear, track shipments, and manage returns.

  • Fast checkout with saved addresses and payment preferences.
  • See your full order history and download invoices.
  • Manage wishlist items and restock reminders.

Guest checkout

Prefer to keep it light? Complete your purchase without creating a password.

  • Simple one-time purchase flow.
  • Receive order confirmations and delivery updates via email.
  • Create an account later with the same email if you change your mind.
Tip: If you train at multiple CMHC schools, registering helps you align delivery addresses and sizes across every order.
