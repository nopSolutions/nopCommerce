Store policy

Conditions of use

Respect for one another keeps our community strong. Please review these guidelines before using the online store.

Using this site

  • Products are intended for personal training and instruction within approved CMHC schools.
  • Do not misuse the site, attempt to interfere with security, or resell without approval.
  • Account holders are responsible for safeguarding their login details.

Orders & pricing

  • Prices may change; confirmed order totals are displayed at checkout.
  • Occasional inventory updates may impact availability; we’ll notify you if an item is delayed.
  • Taxes and shipping are calculated based on your delivery address.

Community standards

  • We reserve the right to cancel orders linked to fraudulent activity.
  • Respectful communication is expected in all interactions with our team.
  • Updates to these conditions will be posted on this page.

Questions? Contact support before placing your order.
