Terms of service for vendors

Clear expectations keep our partnerships strong and focused on serving students.

Quality & safety

  • Products must meet agreed specifications and safety standards.
  • Notify us immediately if any defect or recall risk is identified.
  • Provide updated certifications when materials change.

Fulfilment

  • Lead times and availability should be confirmed before accepting purchase orders.
  • Packaging must protect products during courier transit.
  • Share tracking references promptly after dispatch.

Commercial terms

  • Invoices must match the agreed pricing and taxes.
  • Returns or replacements for faulty items are handled promptly at your cost.
  • Confidentiality applies to CMHC pricing, volumes, and student data.
Alignment first: We’ll review these terms together during onboarding and keep them updated as our partnership grows.
