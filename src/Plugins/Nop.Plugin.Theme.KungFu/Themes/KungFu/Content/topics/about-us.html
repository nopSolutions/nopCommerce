About Chinese Martial Arts & Health Centre

We teach traditional Kung Fu, Tai Chi, and Chi Gung with a focus on discipline, humility, and lifelong learning. Our instructors blend heritage with modern coaching so every student feels welcome on their journey.

Roots you can trust

We preserve classical Southern styles, conditioning, and weapon forms while keeping safety and accessibility at the heart of every class.

  • Certified instructors who actively train and test.
  • Progressive syllabus that honours tradition.
  • Inclusive environment for beginners through instructors.

Beyond the kwoon

Students join a community that values respect and service. We regularly support outreach, seminars, and charity events to keep the spirit of Kung Fu alive.

  • Regional seminars and workshops.
  • Support for coaches and assistant instructors.
  • Opportunities to volunteer and give back.

Your next step

Whether you want to improve health, sharpen skills, or compete, we’ll pair you with a program that meets you where you are.

  • Kung Fu for striking, forms, and weapons.
  • Tai Chi for balance and breathwork.
  • Chi Gung for mobility and recovery.
